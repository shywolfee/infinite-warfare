Controls

Movement: Arrow keys to move, Page Up/Page Down to go up/down.
Jump: Space bar.
Turn: Q to turn left, E to turn right. Hold Shift+Q or Shift+E for 1-degree turning (hold to repeat).
Vertical aim: S to aim up, X to aim down. Hold Shift+S or Shift+X for 1-degree aiming (hold to repeat).
Fire: Left Control or Right Control (if enabled in options).
Use item: Enter on the selected inventory item.
Reload: R to reload. Shift+R to unload magazine.
Ammo check: A to announce ammo status.
Fire mode: Shift+A to cycle fire modes (semi, burst, auto).
Facing: F to announce your current facing.
Coordinates: C to announce coordinates.
Health: H to check health.
Zone: B to announce current zone.
Chat: / to type a message or command.
Commands: Alt+C to open the command menu.
Online players: F1 to see who is online.
MOTD: F2 to view message of the day.
Ping: F3 to ping the server.
Tracking: T to open player tracking menu, W to get track info.
Map info: M to see what is near you, P for nearby players.
Turbolift: Shift+P while standing on a turbolift control panel to choose a level to travel to.
Volume: Home to increase, End to decrease.
Camera: Hold G + arrow keys to explore with the ghost camera.
Inventory: Tab/Shift+Tab to cycle items. K/Shift+K to cycle categories.
Quickbar: Number keys 1-0 to use quickbar slots. Alt+number to bind.
Points of interest: J to list all POIs sorted by distance.
Vehicle controls: O to open/close door, V to enter/exit, UP/DOWN for speed, LEFT/RIGHT to turn, Alt+W for window, Alt+LEFT/RIGHT to switch seats.
Equipment: Alt+E to open equipment menu. Alt+Backspace to unequip a holster.
Biomonitor Bracelet: Shift+U to scan for nearby players (when equipped).
WireNet: Alt+U to toggle automatic healing on/off (when equipped).
Holster: Shift+Backspace to holster or draw a weapon.
Weapon info: Shift+Enter on a weapon in inventory to view stats and attachments.
Developer suite: Shift+D (dev builds only).
Builder: Shift+B to open the builder menu (dev builds only).
Help: Shift+H to view all help entries. Alt+Enter on an item to view its help.
Quickbar view: Shift+I to view weapons in quickbar.
Support: Alt+S to open the support ticket menu.
Game menu: Escape key.
