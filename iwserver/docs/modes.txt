Game Modes

Every map in Infinite Warfare is played as a Free for All. In the lobby, open the Modes tab and choose Free for All, then pick which map you want from the map selection menu.

Available maps:

Battlegrounds: A war-torn island archipelago with a central city ringed by outlying islands and five underground bunkers.

Habitat Alpha: An abandoned alien colony. A large sci-fi map with domes, tunnels, airlocks, and surface paths.

Ghost Town: A sprawling, deserted city. The largest open street map, formerly known as Free for All.

Select a map and you will be deployed directly to it with a fresh loadout.
