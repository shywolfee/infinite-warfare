1. About The Game
Infinite Warfare is an online exyting first person shooter where it's your main goal to kill people and survive. But there is something to the side, there is a duo sistom so you and someone else can fight together. With your duo it's possible to capture bunkers, more about that later. With original sound effects, fast and ballanced gameplay, this game defines its self from allready known first person shooters such as Redspot or The Killer.

2: the menus
2.1: the main menu
This is the first menu you will come across if you start the game, so let's explain the four options in there.
connect to the server: if you have your name set, this will log you in to the game and you can start playing.
change some options: this will bring you to the options menu, more about that later.
check for new updates: this will check if there is a new version available for you to download.
exit the game: this will quit the game and return you to the borring reality.
2.2: the options menu
This is a small menu with ownley three options, so let's explain them shortly.
set your name: just pick a name you want to be known as, think of it like the name sistom of Redspot.
Set your duo mate: here you have to enter the name of the player you want to form a duo with, if you want to fight somebody after you were a duo with this person, you can enter some random words to stop forming a duo with this person.
Go back: returns you to the main menu.

3: gameplay
3.1: killing people
As you spawn on the map, you can choose to start killing or to collect some items first.
Items make a short beeping noise, just walk over them to pick them up.
You can also hit M to look for items around you.
Now let's asoom you have some ammo for weapons or you go with the starting ammo you get from entering the game and you want to kill somebody.
Choose a weapon with the number row or shift +1 till 4 and press A to find out if it has ammo.
Press T to open the tracking menu, find a player and press enter on him/her.
Now press W to hear where this person is and follow the instructions to get there.
To turn, press Q and E and to check your facing press F.
It is also possible to face up and down, you can press s to face up and x to face down.
If you are there, press control to fire your weapon at this person, the person has to be infront and very slightly to the left or right in order to hit him/her.
3.2: item explanation
If you hit tab or shift+tab, you will cycle thrue your inventory.
Onece you found the item of choice, press enter to activate it.
Now I will try to explain all the items as best as I can, so let's go.
empti bucket: an empti bucket wich you can fill on a water tile, if you have 5 filled buckets you can use them to flood the underground tunnels, but more about that later.
weapon suppressors: if you twist them on the specified weapon, the weapon will be more quiet, can be very useful to sneak on people.
Bunker key: if you go to the controlpannel of a bunker, you can activate this to take it over.
smoke bomb: if you activate this one, players can't track you and you can't track them, could be useful for running away if you are about to loos a fight.
Semtex: this is a bomb you can place on the map, when placed, you can activate it with a semtex controler, (boath can be found in a semtex pack).
torpedo: Just an explosive you can launch at players if they are on a water tile, a bit unrealistic but stil funny and powerful to use.
frag grenade: this is a small grenade you can throw at other players, when thrown it will burst in to frags of glass and other sharp material after a small amount of time.
fake item: when placed, it will become an item on the ground called fake.item, when someone picks it up it will explode and cause a fare bit of damage.
health potion: this will give you some hp after drinking it.
bomb vest: if you wear this soft, warm bomb vest, you are a danger to your self and others, after thirty seconds you and everyone around you will be blown up.
ultra health potion: after gaining 5 kills, you will get this potion wich will reset your health to 10000 after drinking it.
life_injection: this item can revive your duomate when he/she has died.
bunker bomb: place this at the controlpannel of your bunker and make everyone in a wide range explode.
duo plane: this is an airplane wich you can ownly control if you are in a duo with someone, it's used to throw bombs down on players and here is how to use it
1. Spawn the plane by pressing enter.
2. If you want to become the pilot of the airplane, press o now, if you want to control the bomb launcher, wait for your duo mate to enter first.
for the pilot:
1. Turn off the breaks by pressing space.
2. now your aircraft is automatically driving, to assend and desend press up and down arrow.
3. Track the player who you want to target and turn to him/her by pressing the left and right arow keys.
for the bomb launcher, just wait until your target is in a range of 20 tiles and drop that bomb by pressing space. To reload, press enter.

3.3: chests
chests are rare objects on the map, and if you find one, you have to stand exactly on its tile and hit O to open it.
There are three types of chests. the duo plane chest, the ammo chest, The grenades and bombs chest, will give you a bomb vest and 5 frag grenades. And there is also the rocks chest, you will get some rocks wich you can use at the ballista of a bunker.
3.4: bunkers
Bunkers are in my opinion the most fun part of the game because you can capture them and defend them against other players, in this part I will explain how that works.
So, first you want to find a bunker, type /bunkers to see a list of bunkers and their coordinates.
When you have reached the bunker you have to find the controlpannel and put a bunker key in to it.
When you have done that the bunker is yours and you can do anything you like with it.
If you have a bunker bomb and you want to activate it, you only have to use it at the controlpannel and a loud alarm sound will play as the timer starts.
An important thing as well is the ballista, it stands infront of a bunker and you can put rocks in to it and it will shoot them in the direction you are facing.
But an other really exyting thing, is that you can flood the tunnels under your bunker with water.
		To do this, find 10 empti buckets and fill them with water.
Then go in to the tunnel of your bunker and find the watertank.
Fill the watertank with buckets by pressing enter on them.
If you put 10 buckets of water in to the watertank, hit O to start flooding and make sure you are in your bunker again.

3.5. Battle Royale
Every now and then, an admin or developer decides to host a match of battle royale where everyone can partisipate by typing /br.
If the admin or dev starts the match you will have to fight each other on an island, and the last one standing wins the battle royale.

3.6. Commands
/changes: check what's new after a server update.
/pm playername message: send someone a private message.
/duomate playername: sets your duo mate without going to the options menu.
/d: duo chat
/bunkers: shows you a list of bunkers
/br: join battle royale
/leave: leave battle royale
/feedback message: leave some feedback to the developers and admins
