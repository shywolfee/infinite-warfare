1. About the Game

Infinite Warfare is an online first-person shooter built for audio gameplay. Your goal is to survive, eliminate other players, and control territory. Team up with a duo partner to capture bunkers, share vehicles, and coordinate strikes. The game features an accessible audio-form interface, 3D positional audio, and a deep weapon and item system.

2. Getting Started

2.1. Main Menu

  • Connect to the server — Enter the lobby and start playing.
  • Register a new account — Create a username, password, and provide an email.
  • Account management — Log in, authorize your account, or reset your password (visible once registered).
  • View the changelog — See what has changed in recent updates.
  • Learn game sounds — Listen to and familiarize yourself with important in-game sounds.
  • Change some options — Open the tabbed options menu.
  • Check for new updates — Download the latest version if available.
  • Exit the game — Quit to desktop.

2.2. Options Menu

Options are organized into three tabs:

TabSettings

2.3. The Lobby

After connecting and logging in you land in the lobby. From here you can:

  • Modes — Choose a game mode to enter.
  • Account Info — View your account details.
  • Players — See who is online.
  • Rewards — View kill-milestone rewards.
  • Leave Lobby — Disconnect and return to the main menu.
  • Press F1 at any time to view the detailed player list.

3. Game Modes

3.1. Battlegrounds

Fast-paced combat on the Archipelago map (1000×1000 tiles). Features a central city with a plaza, city hall, market, armory, barracks, watchtower, hospital, warehouse, and park. Surrounding islands include a fishing village (north), a town (south), a residential area (east), and a mine (west), all connected by four bridges. Five underground bunkers (Alpha through Echo) are linked by a tunnel network.

3.2. Habitat Alpha

An abandoned alien colony environment (1200×1200 tiles). Multiple domes connected by underground tunnels and airlocks for surface travel.

7. Vehicles

Vehicles spawn randomly across the map (5–10 per match). Press O near a vehicle to open its door, then V to enter. Only passengers with their window down can fire weapons. Melee cannot be used from vehicles. Vehicles take 50 damage when crashing into walls. When destroyed, passengers are ejected and take 25% health damage. Stamina regenerates at +5 every 15 seconds while inside a vehicle.

12. Bunkers

  1. Find a bunker (type /bunkers for locations).
  2. Locate the control panel and use a Bunker Key to capture it.
  3. Once captured, you can:
    • Use the ballista in front of the bunker to launch rocks at enemies.
    • Place a Bunker Bomb at the control panel for a wide-area explosion.
    • Flood the tunnels: fill 10 buckets at a water tile, find the water tank in the tunnel, fill it, then press O to flood.
