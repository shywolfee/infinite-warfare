Chainsaw

A gas-powered melee-range weapon.
Range: 4 tiles. Damage: 60 per tick.
Fuel tank: 80 fuel. Full refuel time: 2.5 seconds. Press R to siphon fuel from your canisters up to 100%, or use a canister to fill to a set percentage. Fuel shows as a percentage.
Walk speed: Slow.
Very fast attack rate at close range. Chews through enemies quickly.
