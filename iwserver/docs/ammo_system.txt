Ammo System

Weapons use realistic magazine and ammo mechanics.

Reloading: Press R to reload. If you have both full and partial magazines, a menu lets you choose which to load.
Unloading: Press Shift+R to unload the current magazine back into your inventory.
Ammo check: Press A to hear rounds loaded, ammo type, and reserve count.

Magazine-fed weapons (pistols, rifles, SMGs) consume a magazine item from inventory when reloaded. Revolvers draw rounds from an ammo belt. Shotguns load individual shells from a shell box.

Laser weapons use energy cells and display charge as a percentage. Flamethrower and Chainsaw use fuel canisters and display fuel as a percentage.

Weapons that share the same caliber and magazine capacity share magazine items. For example, the Glock 17 and Beretta 92FS both use 9x19mm magazines.
