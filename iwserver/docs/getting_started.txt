Getting Started

Welcome to Infinite Warfare!

When you spawn, you receive a random loadout of 1 melee weapon, 1 sidearm, and 1 primary weapon, plus health potions and grenades.

Your weapons are automatically assigned to quickbar slots 1 through 5. Press those number keys to draw a weapon or use an item.

Use the arrow keys to move and the control key to fire. Press R to reload when you run out of ammo, and A to check your ammo status.

Tab and Shift+Tab cycle through your inventory. Press J and K to filter by category (All, Weapons, Ammo, Consumables, Equipment).

Press Shift+H at any time for help, or Alt+Enter on an inventory item to learn about it.

Good luck out there!
