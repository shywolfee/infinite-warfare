Molecular Conduit

The molecular conduit is a one-use upgrade for your translocator. Use it from your inventory while you have a translocator, and it permanently raises the translocator's maximum cell capacity by one, letting you store one extra cell for future teleports.

Each use consumes one conduit and adds one to your battery's ceiling. A translocator can be upgraded a maximum of ten times, so a fully upgraded translocator holds ten cells more than a stock one (twenty cells in total). Once you have installed ten conduits into a translocator, any further conduits will report that it is already fully upgraded.

Because there is never a reason to hold more than ten, you can carry at most ten molecular conduits at a time. They can be found in equipment chests.
