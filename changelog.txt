New in 0.457, build 11 (2026-03-20)
Added vehicle system:
  Vehicles spawn randomly on the map (5-10 per map) in 8 types: Jeep, Humvee, Pickup Truck, APC, Buggy, Sedan, Tank, and Van.
  Each vehicle has health, optional armor, a maximum passenger count, and a unique speed.
  Press O within 5 tiles to open or close the vehicle door. Press V to enter or exit the vehicle.
  In a vehicle, UP/DOWN arrows increase or decrease speed (5 speed levels). Shift+DOWN gradually decelerates.
  LEFT/RIGHT arrows turn 90 degrees. Shift+LEFT/RIGHT turns 1 degree for fine steering.
  Alt+LEFT/RIGHT switches between driver and passenger seats. Only the driver controls movement.
  Press Space to roll the window down or up at your seat. Only the driver seat and up to 2 passenger seats have windows.
  You can only fire weapons with the window down, and melee weapons cannot be used from vehicles at all.
  Press H in a vehicle to see vehicle health, armor (if applicable), speed level, seat, and window status before your own health and stamina.
  Stamina restores a small amount every 15 seconds while seated in a vehicle.
  Vehicles take damage when crashing into walls. When destroyed, all passengers are ejected and take 1/4 health damage.
  Armor absorbs incoming damage fully but also reduces vehicle health by 1/4 of the damage taken.
  Nearby vehicles are announced as you walk around the map (within 10 tiles).
