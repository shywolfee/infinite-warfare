Support Tickets

The support ticket system lets you communicate with staff directly from the game.

Press Alt+S during gameplay to open the Support menu. Options:
  Open a new ticket: enter a subject and description. Admins and developers online are notified immediately.
  View my open tickets: browse your currently open tickets. Select one to view details and staff replies.
  View my closed tickets: browse archived tickets and their close reasons.

You can reply to your open tickets to create a conversation with staff. You can also close your own tickets without a reason.

Staff see an additional "View all open tickets" option listing every open ticket. Staff must provide a reason when closing a ticket. Developers can also manage tickets from the Administrator Panel in the main menu without logging into a character.
