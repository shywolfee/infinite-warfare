Bunkers

There are four underground bunkers on the Shattersea map, one beneath each
quarter of Meridian. Each is reached by a hatch and ladder set into the street
above it.

Bunker North: beneath Meridian Plaza (North Meridian).
Bunker South: beneath Elm Road (South Meridian).
Bunker East: beneath Kensington Drive (East Meridian).
Bunker West: beneath Oceanfront Drive (West Meridian).

Each bunker has a ladder entrance, a control panel and item spawns.

Use the /bunkers command in chat to see bunker locations during gameplay.
