Shattersea Map

Shattersea is the coastal city of Meridian, built across one large island in
the Shattersea. It is a 1300 by 1300 map, dense with streets, buildings and
interiors designed for detailed non-visual navigation. Every corridor is walled
so you cannot walk out of it, every building has an entrance so you can never be
trapped, and no walkable tile is left unnamed.

The city is split into four quarters that meet in the middle. The Old Anchorhold
Canal runs east to west across the whole island, dividing the northern quarters
from the southern ones. Four bridges cross it: the Ivory Bridge (west), the
Redmyr Bridge (central-west), the Old Smith's Bridge (central-east) and the
Anchorhold Bridge (east).

North Meridian: six main streets (Kingsway, Meridian Avenue, Beacon Street,
Cathedral Row, Plaza Street and Northgate Road) around Meridian Plaza and its
fountain. Civic core: First Meridian Bank, Meridian City Hall, Meridian
Cathedral, the Meridian Library, Northside Apartments, the Meridian Theatre, the
Kingsway Hotel, the Meridian Police Station and shops. You deploy in Meridian
Plaza.

South Meridian: five main streets (Harlow Street, Sutton Street, Marsh Lane,
Foundry Street and Elm Road), three alleyways (Tanner's, Cinder and Copper), and
two residential cul-de-sacs (Wren Court and Sparrow Close) whose houses have
basements, attics, upper storeys, cellar bulkheads and staircases with railings.
Also a diner, a fire station, a school, a chapel and a clinic.

East Meridian: three main streets (Half-Moon Way, Albeon Way and Kensington
Drive) plus the minor Jameson Close and Darkstone Ramble and two alleys, one
leading into the run-down Hollows neighbourhood. Albeon Way serves Meridian
International Airport: a terminal with a check-in hall, security checkpoint,
departures concourse and four gate lounges, plus a control tower, a hangar, an
apron, a taxiway, a runway and a long-stay car park.

West Meridian: the waterfront. Oceanfront Drive runs from the Ivory Bridge down
to the docks, with four side streets (Wharf, Netmaker, Saltgrass and Gull).
Here you find Meridian Harbour, three fishing piers, the Meridian Docks, the
Fishing Bureau, the Crescent Market (Meridian's largest marketplace), Old Mill's
Park with its ruined mill and cellar, and the Gilded Perch, a closed restaurant
you enter through the service door and can climb down into the basement of.
North of the canal sit Harbourview's warehouses and Cape Meridian Lighthouse.

Four underground bunkers sit beneath the districts, each reached by a street
hatch. Use /bunkers in chat to see their locations.
