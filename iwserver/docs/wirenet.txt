WireNet

The WireNet is a medical equipment item worn in the Medical slot. When equipped and active, it automatically administers a healing surge when your health drops below 20%, restoring 20% of your maximum health.

Usage: The WireNet activates automatically when your health is critical.
Toggle: Press Alt+U to toggle the WireNet on or off. Toggling it off prevents it from activating, allowing you to conserve charges.
Equip: Use the WireNet from your inventory to equip it.
Unequip: Open the equipment menu (Alt+E) and remove it from the Medical slot.

The WireNet has 3 charges. Each time it heals you, one charge is consumed. When all charges are spent, the WireNet is automatically removed from your equipment.
