Vehicles

Vehicles spawn randomly on the map in 8 types: Jeep, Humvee, Pickup Truck, APC, Buggy, Sedan, Tank, and Van. Each has health, optional armor, a maximum passenger count, and a unique speed.

Controls:
  Press O within 5 tiles to open or close the vehicle door.
  Press V to enter or exit the vehicle.
  UP/DOWN arrows increase or decrease speed through 5 speed levels.
  Shift+DOWN gradually decelerates.
  LEFT/RIGHT arrows turn 90 degrees. Shift+LEFT/RIGHT turns 1 degree.
  Alt+LEFT/RIGHT switches between driver and passenger seats. Only the driver controls movement.
  Press Space to roll the window down or up at your seat.

You can only fire weapons with the window down. Melee weapons cannot be used from vehicles. Press H in a vehicle to see vehicle health, armor, speed level, seat, and window status.

Stamina restores a small amount every 15 seconds while seated. Vehicles take damage when crashing into walls. When destroyed, all passengers are ejected and take one quarter health damage. Armor absorbs incoming damage fully but also reduces vehicle health by one quarter of the damage.
