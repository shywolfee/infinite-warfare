Duo System

The duo system lets you pair up with another player as a team.

Setting a duo mate: Open the options menu and enter your duo mate's name, then press Set duo mate.

Duo chat: Use the /d command followed by your message to send a private message to your duo mate. For example: /d cover me

Life injection: When your duo mate dies, you automatically receive a life injection item. Use it near their body to revive them.

Both players must set each other as duo mates for the system to work fully.
