Holsters

Holsters let you carry extra weapons in dedicated equipment slots. You can equip up to two holsters in the Holster 1 and Holster 2 equipment slots.

Holster types:
  Pistol Holster: holds Glock 17, Beretta 92FS, or Desert Eagle.
  .357 Gun Belt: holds the Taurus Model 66 revolver. Stores up to 24 rounds of .357 Magnum.
  .44 Gun Belt: holds the Magnum Revolver. Stores up to 24 rounds of .44 Magnum.
  SMG Sling: holds MP5, UZI, MP7, or P90.
  Rifle Strap: holds HK G36, AK-47, TAR-21, FAMAS, M4A1, SCAR-H, or FN FAL.
  Sniper Carry Case: holds SG-08, AWP, or Dragunov SVD.
  Shotgun Bandoleer: holds Benelli M4, KS-123, or SPAS-12. Stores up to 16 shells of 12-gauge buckshot.
  Knife Sheath: holds the combat knife.
  Sword Scabbard: holds the machete or katana.
  Bat Holster: holds the baseball bat.

Controls:
  Press Shift+Backspace to holster your currently drawn weapon into a compatible equipped holster.
  Press Shift+Backspace with no weapon drawn to draw a holstered weapon.
  Press Alt+E to open the equipment menu.
  Press Alt+Backspace to unequip a holster, returning any stored weapon and ammo to your inventory.

Gun belts and the shotgun bandoleer can store ammunition. Use an ammo belt or shell box from your inventory while a compatible belt is equipped to load rounds into it. When drawing a weapon from a belt with stored ammo, the weapon is automatically reloaded.
