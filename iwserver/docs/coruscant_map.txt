Coruscant Map

Coruscant is the galaxy-spanning city-planet, built as a deeply vertical stack
of city levels. It is a 1600 by 1600 map that rises to a height of 2000 tiles.
You do not deploy at the bottom - you deploy roughly a thousand tiles up, on the
Main Level, and the city climbs far above you and drops far below.

Walk off an unguarded platform edge and you fall to whatever level lies beneath,
which is one way to reach the lower depths. The proper way between levels is a
turbolift: stand on a turbolift's control panel and press Shift+P to pick a
destination.

Levels, from the top down:

The Skydeck: the highest level, with the Coruscant Skyport, its landing apron,
luxury sky-towers and an open observation deck.

The Senate & Federal District: a monumental level of processional boulevards,
the great domed Senate Rotunda, the Republic Executive Building, ministries, the
Republic Archives and a memorial plaza.

The Main Level (deploy): the Coruscant most people picture. Monument Plaza with
the rocky peak of Umate breaking through the pavement, CoCo Town's commerce lanes
and diner, the neon Uscru Entertainment District with the Galaxies Opera House,
Independence Center and its museum, a botanical garden, and the Main Level
Spaceport.

Calacor Heights: a mid-city level of tidy avenues, hab-towers, a market, a clinic
and cantinas.

The Works: a vast abandoned industrial level of cargo sheds, a dead power plant
and a disused freight port. Its maintenance turbolift is the only lift down to
the underworld.

Level 1313, the underworld: a grim shelf that never sees sky, with a black
market, a cantina and crumbling tenements, riddled with gaps that drop to the
surface. Hard to reach on purpose.

The planetary surface: the very bottom, kilometres down, pitch dark and studded
with the colossal pylons that hold the city up. Every fall eventually lands here.

Three turbolifts connect the levels: the Grand Central Turbolift (all levels),
the Spaceport Turbolift (the three upper levels) and the Underlevel Maintenance
Turbolift (The Works, the underworld and the surface).

Select Coruscant from the Free for All map picker in the lobby to deploy here.
