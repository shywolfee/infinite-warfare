1. About The Game
Infinite Warfare is an online first-person shooter where your goal is to survive and eliminate other players. Fight solo or team up with a duo partner to capture bunkers, manage resources, and dominate the battlefield. With original sound effects, deep weapon and equipment systems, and multiple game modes, the game offers a rich accessible FPS experience.

2. Getting Started
Launch the game to reach the main menu. Register an account or log in, then connect to the server. You will enter the lobby where you can view your account info, see online players, and select a game mode.

Game modes:
  Battlegrounds: fight on the Archipelago, a group of five islands connected by bridges with bunkers, a city, villages, and mines.
  Habitat Alpha: fight on a sci-fi colony map with domes, tunnels, and airlocks.

3. Controls
Movement: Arrow keys. Page Up/Page Down for vertical movement. Space to jump.
Turning: Q/E for 45-degree turns. Shift+Q/Shift+E for 1-degree fine turning (hold to repeat).
Vertical aiming: S to aim up, X to aim down. Shift+S/Shift+X for 1-degree aiming.
Fire: Left Control or Right Control (if enabled in options).
Use item: Enter on the selected inventory item.
Reload: R to reload. Shift+R to unload.
Ammo check: A for ammo status.
Fire mode: T to switch weapon firing modes (semi, burst, auto).
Info: F for facing, C for coordinates, B for zone, H for health/stamina/effects.
Chat: / to type a message or command. Alt+C for command menu.
Online players: F1. MOTD: F2. Ping: F3.
Tracker: Shift+Minus/Shift+Equals to navigate categories (Players, Points of Interest, Items, Vehicles). Minus/Equals to move between items. Backspace to track. Shift+Backspace to cancel tracking. N to view tracked location.
Nearby: P for nearby players, J for points of interest.
Inventory: Tab/Shift+Tab to cycle items. K/Shift+K to cycle categories.
Quickbar: Number keys 1-0 to use slots. Alt+number to bind. Shift+I to view quickbar.
Equipment: Alt+E for equipment menu. Alt+Grave Accent to unequip holster.
Holster: Shift+Grave Accent to holster or draw a weapon.
Weapon info: Shift+Enter on a weapon for stats and attachments.
Vehicles: O to open door, V to enter/exit, arrows to drive, Space for window.
Support: Alt+S for support ticket menu.
Help: Shift+H to browse all help topics by category. Alt+Enter on an item for its help entry.
Game menu: Escape key.
Developer suite: Shift+D (dev builds only). Builder: Shift+B (dev builds only).

4. Weapons
The game features over 40 weapons across multiple classes:
  Melee: Machete, Combat Knife, Katana, Baseball Bat.
  Pistols: Glock 17, Beretta 92FS, Taurus Model 66, Magnum Revolver, Desert Eagle.
  SMGs: P90, MP5, UZI, MP7.
  Assault Rifles: HK G36, AK-47, TAR-21, FAMAS F1, M4A1 Carbine.
  Battle Rifles: SCAR-H, FN FAL, M1 Garand.
  LMGs: RPK, M249 SAW.
  Shotguns: Benelli M4, KS-123, SPAS-12.
  Snipers: SG-08, AWP, Dragunov SVD.
  Special: 120mm Mortar, Ballista, Crossbow.
  Laser: Laser Pistol, Laser SMG, Laser Rifle, Laser Sniper, Laser Cannon.
  Heavy: Flamethrower, Chainsaw, Hayvork, Trector Plow.

Weapons use realistic magazine and clip-based ammo. Fire modes (semi, burst, auto) are available on supported weapons. Suppressors and other attachments can be applied to modify stats.

5. Equipment and Items
  Holsters: equip up to two holsters to carry extra weapons in dedicated slots.
  Bomb Vest: a wearable explosive that detonates after 30 seconds.
  Health Potion / Ultra Health Potion: restore health with residual healing buffs.
  Energetic Potions: restore stamina in 4 tiers (red, blue, green, rainbow).
  Frag Grenade, Smoke Bomb, Semtex, Torpedo, Fake Item: throwable ordnance.
  C4: placeable explosive detonated remotely via HyperComp.
  Bunker Key: use at a bunker control panel to capture the bunker.
  Bunker Bomb: place at a bunker control panel for a large area explosion.
  Life Injection: revive your duo mate.
  Translocator: teleport to points of interest using battery cells.
  Binoculars: view your position and nearest points of interest.
  Adrenaline Syringe: doubles movement speed for 30 seconds.
  Repair Kit: restore partial ammo items to full.
  Fortified Ration: restores stamina and health.
  Waypoint Marker: save and navigate back to a map position.

6. Bunkers
Five underground bunkers (Alpha through Echo) are located beneath each island, connected by a tunnel network. Capture a bunker by using a bunker key at its control panel. After capturing your second bunker, you receive a HyperComp for remote structure management including monitoring, alarms, and C4 detonation. Bunkers are released when you die.

7. Vehicles
Eight vehicle types spawn randomly on the map: Jeep, Humvee, Pickup Truck, APC, Buggy, Sedan, Tank, and Van. Enter vehicles to travel faster, but you can only fire ranged weapons with the window down.

8. Duo System
Set a duo mate in the options or with the /duomate command. Duo partners can revive each other with a life injection, communicate via /d chat, and pilot the duo plane together.

9. Support
Press Alt+S to open the support ticket system. Submit tickets to communicate with staff. You can view, reply to, and close your tickets. Staff can manage all tickets.

10. Commands
/changes: view the changelog.
/pm playername message: send a private message.
/duomate playername: set your duo mate.
/d message: duo chat.
/bunkers: list bunker info.
/feedback message: send feedback to staff.

For the full in-game help system, press Shift+H to browse topics organized by category.
