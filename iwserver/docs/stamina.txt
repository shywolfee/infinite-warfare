Stamina System

Stamina represents your physical endurance.
Stamina degrades when using melee weapons, jumping frequently (more than 3 jumps in 25 seconds), and moving or jumping with heavy weapons (high walk time).
Stamina restores naturally every 5 seconds.
You can also restore stamina with energetic potions which come in red, blue, green, and rainbow quality tiers.
Your stamina is shown when pressing H alongside your health.
Low stamina slows your movement and attack speed.
