Turning and Aiming

Horizontal turning:
Q: Snap left to the next cardinal direction (north, northeast, east, etc.).
E: Snap right to the next cardinal direction.
Shift+Q: Turn left by 1 degree. Hold to keep turning.
Shift+E: Turn right by 1 degree. Hold to keep turning.

Vertical aiming:
S: Snap up (to straight forward or straight up).
X: Snap down (to straight forward or straight down).
Shift+S: Aim up by 1 degree. Hold to keep aiming up.
Shift+X: Aim down by 1 degree. Hold to keep aiming down.

Vertical angle ranges from 0 (straight down) through 90 (straight forward) to 180 (straight up).

Press F to announce your current facing and vertical aim.
Mouse turning is also supported when not holding Alt.
