Inventory System

Your inventory holds all your weapons, ammo, consumables, and equipment.

Cycling items: Press Tab to cycle forward, Shift+Tab to cycle backward.
Categories: Press J and K to filter by category: All, Weapons, Ammo, Consumables, Equipment.
Using items: Press Enter on a selected item to use it. Weapons are drawn, consumables are consumed.
Quickbar: Press Alt plus a number key (1 through 0) to bind the selected item to that quickbar slot. Press the number key alone to activate the slot.
Quickbar view: Press Shift+I to see all weapons currently in your quickbar.
Item help: Press Alt+Enter on any item to view its help entry.

When you die, your inventory is dropped as a corpse that other players can loot.
