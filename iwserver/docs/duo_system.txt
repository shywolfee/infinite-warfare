Team System

The team system lets you group up with other players.

Creating a team: Press Alt+T to open the Teams menu and select Create a team. You become the team leader.

Inviting players: Use /teaminvite [name] or the Teams menu to invite a player. Only the team leader can invite.

Accepting invites: When invited, type /teamaccept to join or /teamdecline to decline.

Team chat: Use /T followed by your message to send a message only to your team members. For example: /T cover me

Leaving a team: Use /teamleave to leave your team. If the leader leaves, leadership passes to the next member.

Disbanding: Use /teamdisband to disband the team (leader only). All members are removed.

Listing teams: Use /teams to see all active teams and their member counts.

Life injection: When a teammate dies, all other team members automatically receive a life injection item. Use it near the fallen teammate's body to revive them.
