Flamethrower

A close-range incendiary weapon.
Range: 15 tiles. Damage: 25 per tick.
Fuel tank: 100 fuel. Full refuel time: 3 seconds. Press R to siphon fuel from your canisters up to 100%, or use a canister to fill to a set percentage. Fuel shows as a percentage.
Walk speed: Slow.
Extremely fast firing rate compensates for low per-hit damage. Full automatic only.
