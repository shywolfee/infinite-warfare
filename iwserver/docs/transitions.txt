Transitions

A transition is an interaction point on a map - an airlock, a doorway, a
teleport pad - that moves you from one place to another when you use it. On
Freya's Ascent, the airlock is a transition between the ship's interior and the
surface of Aesir VI.

Using a transition:

1. Stand inside the transition's zone. You will hear the zone announce where you
   are (for example, "the Deck 1 airlock" or "the boarding ramp").
2. Press Shift+L. You are moved to the transition's destination and told what
   happened (for example, "You cycle the airlock and step down the ramp onto
   Aesir VI").

If there is nothing to step through where you are standing, Shift+L tells you so.
Transitions are one-way, so a place you can leave and return to has a transition
at each end. The airlock has one inside the ship (which drops you down onto the
landing field) and one on the boarding ramp outside (which takes you back up
aboard), so you can come and go freely.

The key can be rebound from the key bindings menu (the action is called "Leave
or board ship"); hold Shift together with it to trigger a transition.
