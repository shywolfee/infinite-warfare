Game Modes

Every map in Infinite Warfare is played as a Free for All. In the lobby, open the Modes tab and choose Free for All, then pick which map you want from the map selection menu.

Available maps:

Shattersea: The coastal city of Meridian on one large island, split into four quarters by the Old Anchorhold Canal and four bridges. Streets, sidewalks and curbs, an airport, a harbour with piers and docks, a marketplace, cul-de-sacs of houses, and four underground bunkers.

Coruscant: The galaxy-spanning city-planet, built as a deep vertical stack of city levels from the skydecks at the top down through the Senate District, the Main Level, Calacor Heights, The Works and the underworld to the planetary surface. Travel between levels by turbolift (Shift+P on a control panel) or by falling off an edge.

Ghost Town: A sprawling, deserted city. The largest open street map, formerly known as Free for All.

Select a map and you will be deployed directly to it with a fresh loadout.
