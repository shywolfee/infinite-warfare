Weapon Attachments

Attachments modify your weapon's stats. Use an attachment item from your inventory to attach it to your currently drawn weapon if compatible. Weapons with attachments display a prefix when drawn or browsed in inventory, for example "silenced glock 17". Each weapon slot can hold one attachment.

Suppressors (muzzle slot):
  Gemtech GM-9: fits Glock 17, Beretta 92FS, and Desert Eagle. Compact 9mm suppressor designed for threaded pistol barrels. Slightly increases fire delay.
  SilencerCo Omega 9K: fits MP5, UZI, MP7, and P90. Lightweight, short-bodied 9mm suppressor optimized for submachine guns. Minimal fire delay increase.
  SureFire SOCOM RC2: fits HK G36, AK-47, TAR-21, FAMAS, M4A1, SCAR-H, and FN FAL. Full-size rifle suppressor rated for 5.56 and 7.62 NATO. Slightly increases fire delay.
  Thunder Beast Ultra 9: fits SG-08, AWP, and Dragunov SVD. Precision bolt-action suppressor with minimal point-of-impact shift. Moderate fire delay increase.
Revolvers, shotguns, LMGs, and special weapons cannot be suppressed.

Foregrips (grip slot):
  Vertical Foregrip: fits all SMGs and rifles. Improves weapon handling for faster movement. One per weapon.
  Angled Foregrip: fits rifles only. Reduces fire delay for faster follow-up shots. One per weapon.

Optics (optics slot):
  Red Dot Sight: fits pistols and SMGs. Reflex sight for faster target acquisition.
  Holographic Sight: fits rifles. Projected reticle for faster target acquisition.
  Tactical Scope: fits snipers and battle rifles. Magnified optic that reduces fire delay but slows movement.

Stock (stock slot):
  Extended Stock: fits SMGs. Improves stability for faster movement at the cost of slightly increased fire delay.

Underbarrel (underbarrel slot):
  Laser Sight: fits pistols, SMGs, and assault rifles. Visible laser for slightly faster fire delay.

Press Shift+Enter on a weapon in your inventory to open the weapon info panel showing stats with attachment modifiers applied and a list of attached items. Use the Remove button to detach removable attachments.
