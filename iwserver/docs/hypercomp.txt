HyperComp

The HyperComp is a structure management device automatically awarded when you capture your second bunker. Use it from your inventory to open the management interface.

Features:
  Bunker Status: view who is in your bunkers and their distance from you.
  Toggle Monitor: receive alerts when players enter your bunkers. Each bunker can be toggled individually.
  Toggle Alarm: activate or deactivate alarm sounds inside your bunkers to startle intruders. Per-bunker toggle.
  C4 Management: view placed C4 charges and detonate them remotely.
  Query Bunkers: view a structured list of your owned bunkers with their index, name, coordinates, and monitor/alarm status.

The HyperComp is lost when you die and your bunkers are released.
