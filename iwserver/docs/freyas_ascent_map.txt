Freya's Ascent Map

Freya's Ascent is a starship that has landed on Aesir VI, a verdant forested
world. Unlike the sprawling city maps, this one is tight and enclosed: almost all
of it is the inside of the ship, a maze of corridors and compartments with no
open space.

The ship has a cargo hold and seven decks stacked above it. The cargo hold sits
in the belly of the ship, one level below Deck 1, and is not counted as one of
the seven decks. Deck 1 is the bottom deck and holds the airlock on its north
side; Deck 7 at the top is the bridge.

Every deck has the same shape: an aft (south) junction, a forward (north)
junction, a central spine corridor between them, and a row of sealed
compartments off each side of the spine, each with its own hatch. Deck 1 is
storage and the airlock; Deck 2 is engineering; Deck 3 is crew quarters; Deck 4
is the mess and recreation; Deck 5 is medical and science; Deck 6 is officers and
security (with the armoury and brig); Deck 7 is the bridge.

There is a turbolift at the forward end and another at the aft end of every deck,
and both reach the cargo hold and all seven decks. Stand on a turbolift's control
panel and press Shift+P to pick a deck.

The whole hull is also modelled on the outside, resting on a landing field, so
you can walk right around the ship - the bow, the stern, the port and starboard
hull, the engine nacelles, the landing struts and the boarding ramp. The only
way between the interior and the surface is the airlock: stand in it and press
Shift+L to step down the ramp onto Aesir VI, and press Shift+L on the ramp to
climb back aboard. Because the decks are up above the ground, the airlock drops
you well down the Z as you step outside.

Aesir VI itself is mostly forest. It holds the landing field with the ship, a
river winding through the trees with a log footbridge, a wooded hill you can
climb with a cabin on top, and a small village beside the field with a couple of
settlers' houses, a spaceport control tower and a branch of the Galactic
Interstellar Bureau.

Select Freya's Ascent from the Free for All map picker in the lobby to deploy
here. You start on Deck 1.
