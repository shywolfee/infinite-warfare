Archipelago Map

The Archipelago is the main map, a 1000 by 1000 area consisting of five islands in an ocean connected by bridges.

Central Island: A full city with a central plaza and fountain, city hall, market, armory, barracks, watchtower, hospital, warehouse, and a park.
North Island: A fishing village with three cottages and a dock.
South Island: A town with a town hall, general store, and tavern.
East Island: A residential area with six houses along named streets.
West Island: A mine with an equipment shed, underground mine tunnels, and ore deposits.

Four bridges (north, south, east, west) connect the outer islands to the central island, with fence railings and ambient water sounds.

Five underground bunkers (Alpha through Echo) are located beneath each island. Each bunker has a ladder entrance, control panel, and item spawns. All five bunkers are connected by an underground tunnel network running beneath the ocean floor. The mine on the west island connects to bunker Echo through its own tunnel system.
