Turbolifts

Some maps, like Coruscant, are built as a stack of city levels at very different
heights. Turbolifts are how you travel between those levels.

Using a turbolift:

1. Walk into a turbolift car and stand on its control panel. You will hear the
   zone announce a control panel (for example, "the Grand Central Turbolift
   control panel").
2. Press Shift+P. A menu opens listing every level that lift can reach. Your
   current level is marked.
3. Choose a level and press Enter. The lift sets off; it announces each level it
   passes on the way, and then announces its arrival at the level you chose.

If you are not standing on a panel, Shift+P tells you there is no turbolift here.
Plain P (without Shift) still lists nearby players as usual.

Not every level is served by every lift. On Coruscant, the Grand Central
Turbolift reaches all levels, the Spaceport Turbolift serves the three upper
levels, and the Underlevel Maintenance Turbolift is the only lift down to the
underworld and the planetary surface. Some places can only be reached by riding
a lift - or by falling off an edge into the level below.
