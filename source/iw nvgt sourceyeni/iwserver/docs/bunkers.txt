Bunkers

There are five underground bunkers on the Archipelago map, one beneath each island.

Bunker Alpha: Located at 35, 250, -5 (beneath the central island).
Bunker Bravo: Located at 305, 250, -5.
Bunker Charlie: Located at 300, 10, -5.
Bunker Delta: Located at 300, 490, -5.
Bunker Echo: Connected to the mine on the west island.

Each bunker has a ladder entrance, a control panel, and item spawns. All five bunkers are connected by an underground tunnel network.

Use the /bunkers command in chat to see bunker locations during gameplay.
