Translocator

The translocator is an equipment item that lets you teleport to points of interest on the map. Use it from your inventory to open an interface showing battery level in cells with percentage, followed by all points of interest sorted by distance.

Select a point of interest and press Translocate to teleport there after a five-second power-up sequence. The translocator consumes one cell for every 50 tiles of distance traveled, rounding up.

Use translocator cell items from your inventory to recharge the battery. When using a cell, you are prompted for how many cells to insert.
