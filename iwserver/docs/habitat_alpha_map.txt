Habitat Alpha Map

Habitat Alpha is set on an abandoned colony on a distant world far from Earth.

The map is a large 1200 by 1200 area featuring several city and administrative domes connected by underground tunnels. Each dome has a stairwell leading up to the street or an access corridor into the tunnel network.

Some domes have airlocks that let you travel outside and walk between domes on the surface. When outside, zone descriptions tell you which dome you are near, whether you are along a wall, near an airlock entrance, or on a path between domes.

Select Habitat Alpha from the Modes tab in the lobby to deploy here.
