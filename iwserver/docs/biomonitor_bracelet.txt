Biomonitor Bracelet

The Biomonitor Bracelet is an equipment item worn in the Bracelet slot. When equipped, it allows you to scan for nearby players and view their health status.

Usage: Press Shift+U to activate the bracelet scan.
Equip: Use the bracelet from your inventory to equip it.
Unequip: Open the equipment menu (Alt+E) and remove it from the Bracelet slot.

The scan detects all living players within a 150-tile radius on the same map and reports each player's name and health percentage. The bracelet has a 15-second cooldown between scans.
